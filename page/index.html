Questions you didn't
actually ask.

Will this damage my MacBook?
No. We read the motion sensor, we don't poke it with a screwdriver. The accelerometer was shipped by Apple to protect spinning hard drives in 2005. Nobody told it to retire.
Is it actually free? Like… free free?
Yes. No paid tier. No subscription. No "Pro". No newsletter waiting in the install flow. If you like it, throw a couple bucks at buymeacoffee. Or don't. He'll still scream.
Why is it unsigned? Is it a virus?
Apple's developer signing costs $99/year and Clank! costs $0. The math didn't math. The source is on GitHub — read it before you run it if you're nervous. Gatekeeper will warn you once: right-click → Open → done.
Intel Mac?
Apple Silicon only (M1/M2/M3/M4). Sorry. Building for both was 3× the work for an app that yells.
Can I use my own sounds?
Yes — drop any .mp3 into the sounds folder. Your boss's voice. A cat. A specific Vine from 2014. We support your choices.
Does it phone home / track me / sell my data?
Zero analytics. Zero telemetry. Zero network calls. He lives entirely in your menu bar and screams into the void.
My coworkers hate me now.
Working as intended. Pause button is in the menu bar dropdown. We also accept "sensitivity too high" as a valid complaint — slider's right next to pause.
Will you add a Windows version?
No. Windows laptops don't need this — they already creak, rattle, and grind on their own. And even if they screamed, the fans would drown out every last moan. Sorry.
